Seneca.io Business Card Generator

A couple years ago a friend generated a series of mazes for his new business cards (http://businesssavvysoftware.com/2010/09/01/business-cards/). I thought it was a cool idea. At the time I asked if he was planning on open sourcing his business cards, the response: "_Have you ever heard of open source business cards_". Well, no actually. But let me be the first!

This repo houses the Seneca.io Business Card Generator scripts.

The Seneca.io logo is a hexagon, so I wanted to generate a hexagonal grid. Each cell within the grid can have it's sides open or closed, allowing a maze generator to create random paths within the grid.
